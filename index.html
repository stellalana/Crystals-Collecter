Crystals Collecter!

How to Play

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game
  • By clicking one of the four crystal you will add a specific number of points to your total
  • You win the game by matching your total to the random number
  • You lose if your total exceeds the random number
  • The crystals' values will remain hidden so try to remember!
  • The cyrstals' values will change at the beginning of each new game
